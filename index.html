Real Estate: House Price Prediction

Price prediction of house take many factors into consideration.

Broadly we can divide factors affecting house price in two parts. viz, Micro factors and Macro factors.

Micro factors consider, factors which are local in nature (which will affect price of local real estate market in region or particular house) and Macro factor consider factor which are global in nature (which will affect entire real estate market in country).

Micro factors: This may include below points.

  • Property Location
  • Updates and Upgrades (Maintenance)
  • Inspection Report
  • Neighborhood Comps
  • Appraisal Value
  • And last but not least features of the house

Micro factors: This may include below points.

  • Economic Indicators
  • Interest Rates
  • Investors

Reference: http://resources.point.com/8-biggest-factors-affect-real-estate-prices/

As house price depends on property local we cannot have general solution to predict house price for entire state/country. House price vary heavily from one location to other, hence in this project we are predicting the house price for particular city (Melbourne city in Australia).

The data we have is, contains some of the features listed above, like Property Location, Features of the house (Number of bedrooms, bathrooms, car parking etc.). We also method by which the house was sold and seller name.

As, we can see, data provided does not have all the features listed above, so it is obvious that my predictions would not be 100% accurate, rather no prediction can be 100% accurate in data science, but more quality features we have with us more accurate will be our model.

But, we have to work with whatever data we have, and try to get meaningful results from it.

Lets, talk about data which we have.
